Metem Otomotiv

Metem Automotive Inc. It was established on 19.09.2004 in Istanbul Dudullu. The company, which started its operations with two rubbing machines, developed in a short time and expanded its capacity and machine park, and became able to carry out operations such as CNC machining, Polygon opening, Allen, Gupilya in-house. At the end of 2005, the company moved to its new facility with a closed area of 1,500m2 in Çorlu, first increasing its CNC machining capacity and finally commissioning its fully automatic hot forging facility. It has the capacity to produce 25,000 pieces of purlin (spherical joint) daily in a finished form in its 4500m^2 facility, which was moved to its own land in 2021.
